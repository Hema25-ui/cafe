Dark Chocolate Recipe

Rich Dark Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup cocoa butter
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Melt the cocoa butter and coconut oil in a double boiler over low heat until completely melted.
  2. Stir in the cocoa powder, honey or maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth.
  3. Remove from heat and pour the mixture into molds or a lined baking dish.
  4. Allow the dark chocolate to cool and harden at room temperature or in the refrigerator.
  5. Once set, cut or break into pieces and enjoy!
