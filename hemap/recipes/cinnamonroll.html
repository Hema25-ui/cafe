Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Delicious Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar (for icing)
  • 2 tablespoons milk (for icing)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm milk. Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, and butter. Add the yeast mixture and egg, and knead until smooth.
  3. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface. Spread brown sugar and cinnamon evenly over the dough.
  6. Roll up the dough tightly and cut into 1-inch slices. Place the slices on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown.
  8. Mix powdered sugar and milk to make the icing, and drizzle over the warm rolls.
