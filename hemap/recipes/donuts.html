Donuts Recipe

Classic Donuts

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Oil for frying
  • Powdered sugar or glaze for topping

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast in warm milk and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together sugar, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Add the yeast mixture to the bowl and stir to combine.
  4. Gradually add the flour and salt, mixing until a dough forms.
  5. Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth, about 5 minutes.
  6. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a cloth, and let rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
  7. Roll out the dough and cut into donut shapes using a cutter.
  8. Heat oil in a deep fryer or heavy pan to 350°F (175°C).
  9. Fry the donuts until golden brown, about 1-2 minutes per side.
  10. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels. Dust with powdered sugar or glaze if desired.
