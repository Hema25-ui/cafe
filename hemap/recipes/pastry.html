Chocolate Pastry Recipe

Delicious Chocolate Pastry

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)
  • 1 egg yolk, for egg wash
  • Additional sugar for sprinkling (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, cocoa powder, granulated sugar, and powdered sugar.
  3. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, egg, and vanilla extract.
  5. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until a dough forms. If using, fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. On a floured surface, roll out the dough to about 1/4-inch thickness.
  7. Cut the dough into squares or rectangles and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  8. Brush the tops of the pastries with egg yolk and sprinkle with additional sugar if desired.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the pastries are set and slightly firm to the touch.
  10. Allow to cool on a wire rack before serving.
