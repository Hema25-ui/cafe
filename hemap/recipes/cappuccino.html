Cappuccino Recipe

Classic Cappuccino

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup milk foam
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
  • Cocoa powder or cinnamon (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Brew one shot of espresso and pour it into a cup.
  2. Heat and froth the milk until it is creamy and has a good foam layer.
  3. Pour the steamed milk into the cup with the espresso.
  4. Top with milk foam and garnish with cocoa powder or cinnamon if desired.
