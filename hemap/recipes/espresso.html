Espresso Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 oz of espresso coffee beans
  • Water
  • Sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Grind the espresso coffee beans to a fine powder.
  2. Fill the espresso machine's portafilter with the ground coffee.
  3. Press the coffee firmly and lock the portafilter into the machine.
  4. Start the espresso machine and let the coffee brew.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
