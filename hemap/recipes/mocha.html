Mocha Recipe

Delicious Mocha

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso
  • 1/2 cup hot chocolate
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Whipped cream (for topping)
  • Chocolate shavings or syrup (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Brew one shot of espresso and set aside.
  2. Prepare hot chocolate according to package instructions.
  3. Heat and froth the milk.
  4. In a cup, combine the espresso with the hot chocolate.
  5. Top with whipped cream and garnish with chocolate shavings or syrup.
